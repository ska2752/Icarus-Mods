ska2_HobosStorage
-----------------------------------
Mod Version: 1


## Description:
Select bags for the discerning Hobo. These include the following:

Hobo's Brindle:
A general purpose rucksack to store just about anything a Hobo could need. Don't leave camp without one.

Hobo's Satchel:
On the go meals for the more adventurous Hobo. Don't get the hangries.

Hobo's Butcher Bag:
For all your cuts of meat. Where's the Beef?
